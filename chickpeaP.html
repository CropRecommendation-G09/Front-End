Preparation before sowing

Choose a site with full sun and well-draining soil. Prepare the soil with a light till and add organic matter to improve fertility.Chickpeas are planted directly into the soil without the need for specific hole preparations.

Sowing method

Directly sow chickpea seeds about 5 cm deep, spaced about 20 cm apart.

Best time to sow

The best sowing time is in the autumn, as chickpeas require cooler temperatures to start.

Post-sowing care

Water sparingly, as chickpeas are fairly drought-tolerant. Avoid overhead watering to prevent fungal diseases.

Harvest and Next Steps

Harvest chickpeas when the pods are firm and the plants have yellowed, typically about 100 days after planting.
