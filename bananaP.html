Preparation before sowing

Select a warm, frost-free environment with rich, well-draining soil. Bananas require a lot of sunlight and protection from wind.

Sowing method

Bananas are propagated through suckers, not seeds. Plant the suckers directly into the prepared soil.

Best time to sow

The ideal time to plant banana suckers is during the rainy season when temperatures are consistently warm.

Post-sowing care

Water frequently to keep the soil evenly moist. Apply high-potassium fertilizer regularly. Remove suckers from the base to allow the main plant to thrive.

Harvest and Next Steps

Banana plants usually produce fruit within 9 to 12 months. Harvest when the fruits are plump and the peels begin to split.
